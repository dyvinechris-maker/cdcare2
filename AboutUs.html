CDcare is the smartest way to buy and pay small small with no interest

We grew up in the average Nigerian family where it took painfully saving for a period of time before simple house-hold appliances could be acquired. One of us waited for months for his mother to save for his first laptop and also realized that many Nigerian families also go through the same arduous circle.

He realized that this is a big problem that he wanted to solve. After iterating different solutions to the problem over 10 years, CDcare was founded as the best solution to the Africans inability to easily pay in one lump-sum for items. CDcare is the smartest way to buy in Africa today and we are happy to give Africans the power to own.

Our Structure

CDcare Limited (RC 1844274) manages customer relationships, product listings, and nationwide deliveries.
Emaxie Limited (RC 1615996) builds and operates the CDcare App and technology infrastructure.
Both companies are fully registered in Nigeria and operate together to deliver the CDcare experience you trust.

Our Model in Brief

  1. Customers choose the product they want on the CDcare App or website.
  2. They select a payment plan that fits their budget.
  3. CDcare ships the item at the mid point of selected installment (both in payment and duration).
  4. Customers complete their remaining installment payments after delivery, within the same time frame as if they had paid before delivery.

This “pay small small” model helps users stay debt-free while enjoying genuine, high-quality products.
